Welcome

Xymen Pro is an useful app for the people who struggles to manage more than one WhatsApp on multiple devices. So we came up with this idea - Manage another WhatsApp on the same device. Isn't it cool to have remote access to your another WhatApp. We thought a while then we decided to take it to the next level, so we added 20 more clients to the app. YES 20 CLIENTS. Now you can manage upto 20 clients through a single device. No matter if your device is on Lollipop or Gingerbread, this app will gonna take you to the shore.


Directly start using the app by navigating to How To Use in Navigation Drawer

FEATURES:
  • You can mange upto 20 clients.
  • Navigation Bar having some awesome features.
  • Hide Action Bar to gain some extra space to get your hands properly on smaller devices.
  • Hide Navigation Bar to get even some space :)
  • Provided FAQ section which will have the answer to your little questions.
  • Provided FeedBack section so that you can let us know how you like the app and provide some feedback or to ask questions.

TIPS:
  • Give some time to the page to load. It is quite a heavy page. Especially for 2G subscribers.
  • Prefer Wifi because of the same above reason.
  • Try to use in landscape mode. It's a bit comfortable in that way.
  • And remove Action Bar. It makes it even more comfortable :)
  • The App is in its BETA version so can lags sometimes. But not much just HAVE PATIENCE.
