How To Use

First Time
  1. Make sure you have internet connetion in both the phones - This One and Client's one.
  2. Open Client Drawer by swiping from the right.
  3. Click Client 1
  4. Let the page load completely.
  5. Find the QR Code on the page.
  6. OPTIONAL: Fit it on the screen by pinch zooming.
  7. Now open client's WhatsApp.
  8. Click Settings(Three Dots in Upper Right Corner).
  9. Select WhatsApp Web
  10. Click OK, Got it.(If you're opening it for the first time)
  11. Now scan the QR code which you found in the step 3.
  12. You're done. Enjoy using two or more than two WhatsApp on your phone :)
  13. Pinch Zoom To fix it on your screen, to read small text, navigate through the stuff, reply etc..

Adding More Clients
  1. Open Client Drawer by swiping from the right.
  2. Click Add New
  3. Click on the newly added client i.e Client 2 for the fist time.
  4. The Client will start downloading. Wait for it to complete.
  5. Install the app.(IMP:If you don't install it. You can't use more clients.)
  6. Navigate back to the app and click on the client you installed just now.
  7. Now Setup this client by following the steps in FIRST TIME section.
  8. Enjoy More than 1 WhatsApp on a single device.
